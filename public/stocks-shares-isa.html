Stocks & Shares ISA

A tax-efficient investment account allowing you to invest in funds, shares, bonds, and more. Potential for growth is higher than cash savings — but so is the risk. This page is for information only and not financial advice.

How It Works

  • You can invest up to £20,000 each tax year (combined across all ISAs).
  • Any growth, dividends, or interest earned inside the ISA is free from UK income and capital gains tax.
  • Investments can go up or down in value — you might get back less than you put in.
  • You can withdraw money at any time (though this may reduce long-term growth potential).

ISAs must be held in your own name and are available to UK residents aged 18 or over.

Why People Use Stocks & Shares ISAs

  • To invest tax-efficiently for long-term goals (5+ years).
  • To build wealth without paying capital gains or dividend tax.
  • As part of a retirement strategy (often alongside pensions).
  • For future goals such as children’s education or home deposits.

Considerations (Information Only)

  • Investing is best for long-term horizons — markets fluctuate in the short term.
  • Diversification (spreading investments) can help reduce risk.
  • Charges vary between providers and funds — these can affect returns.
  • Past performance isn’t a guide to future returns.
