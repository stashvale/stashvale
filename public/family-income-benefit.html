Family Income Benefit

Information only — not financial advice.

Family Income Benefit (FIB) pays a tax-free income to dependants for the remaining term if the insured person dies during that term.

Key Points

  • Term alignment — often aligned to youngest child’s independence age.
  • Indexation — option to increase payments with inflation.
  • Budgeting — income can be easier to manage than a lump sum.
