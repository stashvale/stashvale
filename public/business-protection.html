Business Protection

Information only — not financial or tax advice.

Business protection helps a company manage financial impact if key people die or suffer critical illness. Structures and tax treatment depend on circumstances; specialist advice is essential.

Key Areas

  • Key person — helps cover costs or losses tied to a key individual.
  • Shareholder/partnership — arrangements to buy shares if an owner dies.
  • Relevant Life — tax-efficient life cover for employees in some cases.
