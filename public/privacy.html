We collect your name, contact details (email/phone), general product interests, your preferred contact time, and any free-text enquiry you choose to provide. We ask you not to include medical details or policy numbers.

International transfers

Some processors may store data outside the UK/EEA with appropriate safeguards (e.g., standard contractual clauses).

Complaints
