Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 September 2025

Who we are: Stashvale (“we”, “us”). Contact: info.stashvale@gmail.com.

What data we collect

We collect your name, contact details (email/phone), general product interests, preferred contact time, and any free-text enquiry you choose to provide. Please do not include medical details or policy numbers via this website.

Why we use your data (lawful basis: consent)

  • To respond to your enquiry and contact you about protection products and related services.
  • If you opt in, to email you when we publish a new blog post or send occasional updates (you can unsubscribe at any time).

How long we keep your data

We keep enquiry data for up to 24 months from last contact, unless you withdraw consent earlier or ask us to delete it. Marketing preferences are kept until you unsubscribe.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We use processors that help us run the website (e.g., Netlify for hosting/forms, email providers). If our business or client book is sold, your data may transfer to the buyer under the same privacy terms. We do not sell or license individual enquiry records to unrelated third parties for their own marketing without your explicit, separate consent.

International transfers

Some processors may store data outside the UK/EEA with appropriate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses/UK Addendum).

Your rights

  • Access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, and withdrawal of consent.
  • To exercise these rights, email info.stashvale@gmail.com.

Complaints

You can complain to the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) if you’re unhappy with how we handle your data.

This website provides information only and does not provide regulated financial advice.
