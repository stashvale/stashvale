Whole of Life Insurance

Information only — not financial advice.

Whole of life insurance aims to last for life; premiums may be guaranteed or reviewable. It’s sometimes used for funeral planning or as part of estate planning.

Key Points

  • Guaranteed vs reviewable — premium stability vs reassessment.
  • Trusts — may help proceeds reach beneficiaries efficiently.
  • IHT context — can support wider planning with professional advice.
