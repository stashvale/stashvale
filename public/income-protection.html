Income Protection

Information only — not financial advice.

Income protection pays a monthly benefit if illness or injury stops you working, after a chosen deferred period.

Key Types & Concepts

  • Deferred period — how long before benefits start (e.g., 4–26 weeks).
  • Short vs full-term — limited claim length vs claims to a set age.
  • Definitions — “own occupation” vs broader definitions affect claims.
