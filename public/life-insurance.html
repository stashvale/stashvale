Life Insurance

Information only — this page is general guidance and not financial advice.

Life insurance pays a benefit if the insured person dies during the policy term. People commonly use it to help family maintain living standards or to cover debts.

Key Types & Concepts

  • Level term — payout stays fixed; often used for family security or interest-only mortgages.
  • Decreasing term — payout reduces over time to mirror a repayment mortgage.
  • Family Income Benefit (FIB) — pays income over remaining term instead of a lump sum.
  • Term length & amount — align with your needs and obligations; amount is personal.
  • Joint vs single — decide whether one policy or two single policies suits your household.
