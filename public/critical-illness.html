Critical Illness Cover

Information only — not financial advice.

Critical illness cover pays a lump sum if you are diagnosed with a listed serious condition according to policy definitions.

Key Types & Concepts

  • Covered conditions — often major cancers, heart attack and stroke (definitions vary).
  • Partial payments — some policies include part payments for less severe events.
  • Children’s cover — optional extras may be available.
