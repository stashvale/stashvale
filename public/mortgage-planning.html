Mortgage & Property Planning (Information Only)

A neutral overview of the typical steps in a purchase or remortgage and the key terms people often encounter. This page is not mortgage advice or a recommendation.

Key Terms

  • LTV (Loan-to-Value): The size of the loan as a percentage of the property value.
  • Repayment vs Interest-Only: Whether monthly payments reduce the balance or mainly cover interest.
  • Fixed/Variable/Tracker: How interest rates may be set and how they might change.

Typical Stages (High Level)

  • Budgeting and understanding regular costs (mortgage payments, insurance, maintenance, taxes/fees).
  • Agreement in principle (AIP), property search, valuation and survey (where applicable).
  • Legal work (conveyancing), contracts, exchange and completion.

Neutral Considerations

  • Affordability and sustainability of payments, especially after any initial rate period ends.
  • Fees, product charges and any early repayment charges.
  • Insurance requirements set by lenders and other ongoing property costs.

For personal mortgage recommendations, consider speaking to a suitably qualified, regulated adviser.
